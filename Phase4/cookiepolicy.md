
# Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how our Booking System ("we", "us", or "our") uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit and use our web application.

### What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your web browser. They help websites remember your preferences, login status, and other information to improve your experience.

### Types of cookies we use

- **Essential cookies:** Required for the website to function (e.g., session management, authentication).
- **Performance cookies:** Collect anonymous data on how users interact with the site to help us improve functionality.
- **Functionality cookies:** Remember your preferences and settings.
- **Security cookies:** Help detect and prevent security risks.

### How we use cookies

We use cookies to:

- Authenticate users and maintain sessions
- Remember your preferences (such as language)
- Enhance security and prevent fraud
- Analyze usage to improve our service

### Third-party cookies

We do not use third-party advertising cookies. Some analytics or embedded content may set their own cookies, subject to their privacy policies.

### Managing cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect the functionality of the booking system.

### Changes to this policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
