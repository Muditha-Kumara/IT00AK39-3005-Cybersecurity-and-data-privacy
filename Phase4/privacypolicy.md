
# Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how our Booking System ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal data in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

### What data do we collect?

We collect only the minimum personal data necessary to provide our service, including:

- Name, email address, username
- Age (to verify eligibility for booking)
- Booking and resource usage data

### How do we use your data?

We use your data to:

- Register and authenticate users
- Manage bookings and resources
- Enforce age restrictions
- Communicate important information
- Improve system security and performance

### Legal basis for processing

We process your data based on your consent (given at registration), contractual necessity, and our legitimate interests in providing a secure booking system.

### Data sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties except as required by law or to provide our service (e.g., hosting providers under strict confidentiality).

### Data retention

Personal data is retained only as long as necessary for the purposes described above or as required by law. You may request deletion of your account and data at any time.

### Your rights

You have the right to:

- Access your personal data
- Correct inaccurate data
- Request deletion (right to be forgotten)
- Withdraw consent at any time
- Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

### Data security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including encryption, access controls, and regular security reviews.

### Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
